# Disclaimer

## About This Website

The information presented on this website was gathered through verbal recollections from living members of the Souvannavong family residing in Minnesota. Photographs were provided by family members in Australia.

This oral history and photographic evidence were then researched and expanded using **ChatGPT 5.2** to provide historical context and additional details.

## Verification Status

While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, **not all information has been independently verified by professional historians or authoritative sources**.

The content on this website should be understood as:

- Family oral history and recollections
- Personal photographic archives
- AI-assisted historical research and contextualization
- Preliminary documentation awaiting scholarly verification

## Recommendations for Researchers

Readers are encouraged to:

- Verify critical details through primary historical sources
- Consult professional historians specializing in Lao history
- Cross-reference information with international archival sources
- Treat this website as a starting point for further research

For corrections, additional information, or verification inquiries, please [contact the Souvannavong family](/contact/).
