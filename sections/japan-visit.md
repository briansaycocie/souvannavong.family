# Section VI: The 1970 Royal State Visit to Japan

From **11 to 15 August 1970**, King Savang Vatthana and Queen Khamphouy undertook an official visit to Japan, hosted by the Government of Japan and coinciding with **Expo '70 in Osaka**.

![Japanese government commemorative souvenir document for the 1970 Royal Visit of King Savang Vatthana and Queen Khamphouy to Japan](/assets/images/1970-Royal-Visit-Japanese-Government-Commemorative-Souvenir-Document.jpg)

Family-held materials include a French-language commemorative folder issued by the Japanese government confirming the visit's dates and official status.
