# Section IX: Collapse of the Kingdom and Exile (1975)

In **1975** (age 77), the monarchy was abolished. Royal advisors, senior officials, and military officers were targeted for imprisonment or re-education.

As President of the King's Council, Outhong Souvannavong was at immediate risk and fled Laos.
