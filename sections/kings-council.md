# Section V: President of the King's Council and Royal Advisor (1965-1975)

In **1965** (age 67), Outhong Souvannavong was formally installed as **President of the King's Council (Privy Council)**.

![Outhong Souvannavong advising King Savang Vatthana as President of the King's Council](/assets/images/Outhong-Souvannavong-Advising-King-Savang-Vatthana.jpg)

This position made him:

- the King's principal constitutional advisor
- presiding officer of the upper legislative body
- one of the most powerful non-royal figures in the Kingdom

Only individuals with exceptional trust and seniority were appointed to this role.
