# Section X: Diaspora Across Three Continents

## France

France served as an initial refuge for senior Lao officials due to language and diplomatic ties.

## Australia

Outhong later relocated to **Australia**, where he lived in quiet exile until his death in **March 1996**. Family visits there preserved royal-era photographs and documents.

![Memorial headstone: In Memory of Chao Phagna Luang Muong Chanh Outhong Souvannavong, 23 September 1898 to 12 March 1996](/assets/images/In-Memory-Of-Chao-Phagna-Luang-Muong-Chanh-Outhong-Souvannavong.jpg)

## United States - Minnesota

Another branch of the immediate family, including the user's mother, fled to the **United States**, settling in **Minnesota**, a major Lao refugee resettlement center. The family remains there today.
