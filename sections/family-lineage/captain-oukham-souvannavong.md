# Section VIII: Captain Oukham Souvannavong - Royal Lao Air Force (1960s)

![Captain Oukham Souvannavong](/assets/images/Captain-Oukham-Souvannavong.jpg)

Captain Oukham Souvannavong, son of Outhong Souvannavong, served in the **Royal Lao Air Force** as a **C-47 flight engineer and aircraft mechanic**.

The C-47 Skytrain was used for senior transport, logistics, and strategic missions. The role required advanced technical training, security clearance, and institutional trust.

His service reflects the continuation of the Souvannavong family's trusted role within the Lao state.
